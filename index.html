The New Standard of Luxury: Mahindra Marina 64

Mahindra Lifespaces is set to redefine Malad West with their upcoming premium project, Mahindra Marina 64. Located at Liberty Garden Navy Nagar, this luxurious development offers spacious 2, 3, and 4 BHK apartments, designed for modern comfort and elegance. About Mahindra Lifespaces

Since 1994, Mahindra Lifespaces has been a leader in Indian real estate, embodying the Mahindra Group’s “Rise” philosophy by creating exceptional living and working spaces. Offering premium residences through Mahindra Lifespaces and affordable homes via Happinest, they meet diverse needs across India.

Renowned for quality construction, sustainability, and vibrant communities, Mahindra Lifespaces is a trusted name in the Indian real estate market. Their projects promote economic growth and social development, ensuring a high level of accountability and trust, making them a preferred choice for investors and homeowners seeking quality and future-ready living spaces.
