About Mahindra Lifespaces

Since 1994, Mahindra Lifespaces has been a leader in Indian real estate, embodying the Mahindra Group’s “Rise” philosophy by creating exceptional living and working spaces. Offering premium residences through Mahindra Lifespaces and affordable homes via Happinest, they meet diverse needs across India. Their Mahindra World City (MWC) projects promote economic growth and social development, while ORIGINS by MWC supports businesses with well-equipped industrial townships. Renowned for quality construction, sustainability, and vibrant communities, Mahindra Lifespaces is a trusted name in the Indian real estate market.
